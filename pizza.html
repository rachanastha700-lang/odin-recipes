Pizza

Description

To make a simple cheese pizza, you only need 4 ingredients : flour, olive oil, sauce and cheese. After you master the basics of making a simple cheese pizza, this there is an infinite amount of recipes that you can create by adding any ingredients on the pizza base. The base sauce can be anything like classic marinara, pesto, alfredo, garlic and spicy arrabbiata or no sauce at all. Cheese can be mozzarella, provolone, cheddar, parmesan and gorgonzola as well based on preference. No cheese can be done as well for people who dont like it or people who are lactose intolerant.

Ingredients

  • Bread flour
  • salt,sugar
  • olive oil
  • instant/rapid rise yeast
  • warm water
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • garlic
  • dry oregano
  • red pepper flakes
  • cheese of your liking e.g. mozerella
  • pizza toppings of your liking e.g. Pepperoni, Bacon, sausage, mushroom, tomato, pepper, chillies, basil, olive, chicken, asparagus

Steps

  1. Make the dough: Mix flour, water, oil, salt, sugar, yeast together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer.
  2. Knead: Knead by hand or with your mixer. I like doing this by hand.
  3. Rise: Place dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Punch & shape: Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle. Cover and rest as you prep the pizza toppings.
  5. Top it: Top with favorite pizza toppings and cheese.
  6. Bake: Bake pizza at a very high temperature for only about 15 minutes.
  7. Now you have fresh homemade pizza made with love!!

Onion Rings

Description

An onion ring is a popular snack or side dish. It's made by cutting an onion into circular slices,separating the rings,dipping them in batter or breadcrumbs,deep-frying them until golden and crispy They're usually crunchy on the outside, soft and slightly sweet inside, and often served with dips like ketchup or mayo. You'll commonly see onion rings in fast-food places or as a side with burgers.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • all purpose flour
  • cornflour
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • water or milk
  • breadcrumbs
  • oil

Steps

  1. Slice onions: Cut onions into thick round slices and separate them into rings.
  2. Make batter: In a bowl, mix flour, cornflour, salt, pepper, and a little water or milk to make a smooth (not too thin) batter.
  3. Heat oil: Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  4. Coat the rings: Dip each onion ring into the batter.
  5. Fry: Carefully put the rings into hot oil. Fry until golden brown.
  6. Drain oil: Take them out and place on tissue paper to remove extra oil.
  7. Serve hot: Enjoy with ketchup or mayo!!
